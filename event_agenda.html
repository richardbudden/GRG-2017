Schedule of Event

Friday's Schedule:

  • 4:00pm - Pits Open/Robot Drop-off
  • 5:00pm - Practice Matches Begin and
    Orientation for New Students to FRC (Field,Pits,etc)
  • 8:00pm - Practice Matches End
  • 9:00pm - Pits Close

Saturday's Schedule(Competition Day):

  • 7:00am - Pits are Open
  • 8:00am - "Ladies in Tech" Career Panel Discussion
  • 8:45am - Opening Ceremonies
  • 9:00am - Matches Begin
  • 12:30pm - Lunch
  • 1:15pm - Matches Continue
  • 2:30pm - Drafting for Final Elimination Tournament
  • 3:00pm - Tournament Begins
  • 5:00pm - Awards Ceremony
  • 5:30pm - Event Concludes
  • (Robots may be dropped off Friday evening, between 6:00 - 9:00 PM)
